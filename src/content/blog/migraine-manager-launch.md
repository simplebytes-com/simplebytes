We're happy to introduce [Migraine Manager](https://migrainemanager.com), a new Simple Bytes product: a fast, calm, private migraine tracker built for the exact moment symptoms start.

## Built for the Moment Pain Strikes

Most tracking apps assume you can sit down and fill out a form. When a migraine hits, you often can't even look at your screen. Migraine Manager is designed for that reality.

The core idea is simple: **start a log now, add the details later.** One-tap Attack Mode starts the timer instantly, with low-light, soft-contrast screens that are comfortable to use mid-attack. When symptoms ease, you go back and add severity, symptoms, medication, and any suspected triggers.

## What It Does

- **One-tap Attack Mode** — Start a log the instant an attack begins, no typing needed.
- **Low-light, calm screens** — Designed for logging even when light hurts.
- **Medication tracking** — Log doses, timing, and relief while keeping an eye on your medication days.
- **Trends & correlations** — See attack frequency, duration, and severity over time, and surface the patterns that repeat.
- **Clinician-ready exports** — Turn months of logs into a clear summary to bring to your neurologist.
- **Home & Lock Screen widgets** — Start a log or check your week without opening the app.

## Private by Default

Migraine Manager works fully on-device. There are no accounts required to get started, and your history stays with you. Optional sync backs up across your devices for Pro users who want it.

## Pricing

The core experience — attack logging, basic local history, and water tracking — is free. Pro unlocks sync, deeper analysis, widgets, and clinician-ready exports: $2.99/week, $29.99/year (with a 7-day free trial), or $99.99 for lifetime access.

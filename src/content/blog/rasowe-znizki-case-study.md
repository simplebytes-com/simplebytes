Simple Bytes has entered into a partnership with [Rasowe Zniżki](https://rasoweznizki.pl), a fast-growing community and discounts platform for pet owners in Poland. We've joined as a **minority partner**, providing the technology and product consulting behind the product while the brand and business stay firmly in the hands of its founder.

This is a different kind of engagement for us. We're not acquiring or operating Rasowe Zniżki — we're building it.

## What is Rasowe Zniżki?

Rasowe Zniżki is an app and community for conscious pet owners — dogs, cats, and horses — who want verified brands and genuine savings in one place. It brings together curated discounts and coupons from trusted partners, alongside education, events, and an ambassador program.

The positioning is deliberately broad: it serves pedigree and mixed-breed owners alike, with a focus on *education, not marketing*. It's less a coupon dump and more a community for people who care about doing right by their animals.

## Our Role

As the technology and consulting partner, Simple Bytes delivered the full product stack and continues to advise on roadmap and commercialization:

- **Mobile apps** for iOS and Android, built with Expo and React Native
- **A backend API** on Fastify and PostgreSQL, with type-safe data access end to end
- **A partner portal** for brands to onboard, manage assets, and see performance
- **A content CMS** so the team can manage events, posts, and media without engineering
- **A marketing site** at [rasoweznizki.pl](https://rasoweznizki.pl), built with Astro and deployed on Cloudflare

The goal was a polished, mobile-first launch paired with the lightweight tooling Rasowe Zniżki needs to sell, manage, and prove value to pet-brand partners across seasonal campaigns.

## Why This Partnership

Rasowe Zniżki fits the Simple Bytes philosophy — a focused product solving a real, everyday problem for a community that genuinely cares. As a minority partner, we get to do what we do best (build and ship great software) while a passionate founder leads the brand, the partnerships, and the community.

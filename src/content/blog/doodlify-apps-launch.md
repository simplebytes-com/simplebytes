Doodlify just took a big step: it's now available as native apps on both [iOS](https://www.doodlify.ai) and Android.

## Doodles, Now in Your Pocket

[Doodlify](https://www.doodlify.ai) turns your photos into cute, 2D flat and colorful doodles with AI — no artistic ability required. Until now, that magic lived on the web. Today, it lives on your phone too.

The native apps bring the full Doodlify experience to mobile, so you can transform photos straight from your camera roll wherever you are.

## What You Can Do

- **Upload any photo** — Portraits, pets, couples, and family pictures all work beautifully.
- **Transform with AI** — Get a charming, colorful doodle in seconds.
- **Save & share** — Download high-resolution results for profile pictures, gifts, and creative projects.

## Get the Apps

Doodlify is available on the [App Store and Google Play](https://www.doodlify.ai). The web version remains available too — pick whichever fits how you create.

We're excited to put Doodlify in more hands. Give it a try and turn your favorite photos into doodles.

We're excited to announce that Simple Bytes has acquired [HyperTxt.ai](https://www.hypertxt.ai) — and that, after a full rebuild, HyperTxt relaunched on June 1, 2026. You can also read the Simple Bytes portfolio page for [HyperTxt.ai](/projects/hypertxt).

## What is HyperTxt?

HyperTxt is an AI brand visibility engine for search workflows. It turns your brand knowledge, keywords, and optional research sources into publish-ready SEO drafts for the channels you already use.

Instead of generic AI output, HyperTxt learns from your actual brand materials — text, website URLs, and sitemaps — so every draft stays on-voice. It pairs that with provider-backed keyword metrics and a multi-pass writing workflow that moves from outline to draft to review.

## Why HyperTxt?

HyperTxt fits the Simple Bytes mission of building focused, genuinely useful tools. Most AI writing tools either fabricate keyword data or churn out content that reads nothing like your brand. HyperTxt takes the opposite approach:

- **Brand-aware generation** — Learns from your real brand materials, not just a prompt.
- **Provider-backed keywords** — Real metrics from DataForSEO and Keywords Everywhere, never invented.
- **Multi-pass drafts** — Outline, draft, and review passes produce human-quality, publish-ready articles.
- **Export & publish** — Markdown export plus WordPress, Ghost, and webhook integrations.

## Rebuilt From the Ground Up

We didn't just take HyperTxt over and leave it running. We rebuilt it on a new full-stack foundation — a production-first backend with encrypted integration secrets, versioned prompt workflows, and support for both hosted and bring-your-own-keys operation. That's why we call June 1 a relaunch, not just an acquisition.

## Pricing

HyperTxt is available from $19/month, with every feature available on every plan — no feature gating. There's also a bring-your-own-keys plan for teams that want to run on their own provider API keys.

## What's Next

HyperTxt continues at [hypertxt.ai](https://www.hypertxt.ai). We're focused on expanding publishing integrations and sharpening the multi-pass workflows. We're thrilled to welcome it to the Simple Bytes family.
